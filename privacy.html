Privacy Policy

Operator: ElevixApps

Last updated: March 19, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how ElevixApps (“we”, “us”, or “our”) handles information when you use Retainer Pulse.

By installing or using Retainer Pulse, you acknowledge this policy. If you do not agree, do not use the app.

2. Information we collect

We collect only what is reasonably needed to provide and operate the app:

  • Account identifiers from monday.com — such as your monday.com user and account identifiers, and basic profile details monday.com makes available to installed apps, so we can authenticate you and know which workspace the app is used in.
  • monday.com content you interact with in the app — such as board, item, and subitem data (including column values on those records) needed to read your source subitems, show a preview, and create subitems on your selected targets. We do not ask for broader access than is necessary for these actions.
  • App configuration you create — for example, board-level template data you save inside the app, so you can reuse it.
  • Limited technical information — such as standard server or application logs (e.g. timestamps, error messages) used to keep the service reliable and secure.

3. How we use information

We use information solely to provide, operate, secure, and support Retainer Pulse — for example, to authenticate requests, perform the actions you initiate, troubleshoot issues, and meet legal requirements. We do not use your monday.com data for unrelated purposes.

4. monday.com data access

Retainer Pulse works inside monday.com. Access to your monday.com account and boards is granted through monday.com’s installation and permission model. The app is designed to use the minimum data needed to support its core functionality. Exact permissions shown in monday.com at install or in your account settings control what the platform exposes to the app.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may disclose information only as needed to run the service:

  • monday.com — data is processed through monday.com’s platform and APIs when you use the app.
  • Infrastructure and service providers — such as hosting, logging, or security vendors we use to operate the app, who process data on our instructions and only as needed to provide the service.
  • Legal and safety — if required by law, or to protect our rights, users, or the security of the service.

6. Data retention

We retain information only as long as needed to operate Retainer Pulse, comply with law, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Technical logs may be kept for a limited period for security and reliability. If you remove the app or delete data in monday.com, residual retention may still apply for backups or logs for a short time as part of normal operations.

7. Data security

We use appropriate technical and organizational measures designed to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure; we work to follow common industry practices appropriate to the nature of the service.

8. Your rights and choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict certain processing of your personal information, or to object to certain processing. You can contact us using the details in Contact us below. You can also adjust or revoke the app’s access through your monday.com account settings, and uninstall the app if you no longer wish to use it.

9. Third-party services

Retainer Pulse relies on monday.com and on subprocessors (such as cloud hosting) that help us deliver the app. How monday.com handles account and workspace data on its platform is described in monday.com’s terms and privacy notices. How our hosting and other providers handle data is covered in their respective policies. This policy describes our app’s processing as set out above.

10. Children’s privacy

Retainer Pulse is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please reach us through our Support page and we will take appropriate steps.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised version on this page and update the “Last updated” date. If changes are material, we will provide notice in a reasonable way when practical (for example, by posting an update on this site). Your continued use of the app after the effective date of changes means you accept the updated policy.

12. Contact us

For privacy-related questions or requests, use our Support page and submit the form there. That is the best way to reach ElevixApps about this policy or your personal information.
