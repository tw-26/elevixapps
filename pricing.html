Frequently asked questions

What counts as a subitem creation?

Each time the app creates a new subitem under an item—whether by cloning from a source item or applying a template—it counts as one subitem creation. Editing or updating existing subitems does not count toward your limit.

How is usage measured?

Usage is tracked per monday.com account per calendar month. For example, if you create 500 subitems in March, that’s 500 toward your March limit. Counts reset at the start of each new month.

What happens if I reach my limit?

When you hit your plan’s monthly subitem limit, you won’t be able to create more subitems with the app until the next month or until you upgrade. You’ll see a clear message in the app when you’re approaching or at your limit.

How does billing work?

Subitems Multiplier is billed through monday.com. When you upgrade to Pro or Business, the charge appears on your monday.com subscription. You can change or cancel your plan from your monday.com account settings.
